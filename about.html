About MyHealthID

Our Mission & Vision

Who We Are

MyHealthID is a revolutionary healthcare platform designed to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare providers. Our mission is to make quality healthcare accessible to everyone through secure digital solutions.

Founded in 2024, we've been committed to transforming how people manage their health and access medical services.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every person has instant access to their complete health history and can connect with healthcare professionals anywhere, anytime.

Through innovation and technology, we're creating a healthier, more connected world.
